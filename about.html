About Hongliang Tech

Hongliang Tech is a manufacturing-focused supplier of extrusion and injection screws, barrels and liners for industrial plastic processing applications.

We specialise in OEM replacement and drawing-based production, supporting customers who require stable performance, durable components and reliable delivery.

Manufacturing-focused supplier We produce extrusion screws, barrels and liners based on application requirements, processing materials and technical drawings.
